Joshua Ngo is a designer and creative based in Seoul, South Korea. His practice sits at the intersection of research, design, and craft — guided by the belief that meaningful work begins with careful observation.

Each project is an exercise in restraint: stripping away the unnecessary until only the essential remains. The aim is always clarity — work that communicates honestly and serves its purpose without excess.
